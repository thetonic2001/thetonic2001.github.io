About

Our Mission


to provide entertainment and information in a unique and interesting way that utilises social activity.


At Solid Robot, we come to work every day to try and enhance the world's experience of the internet. The internet is great. It provides unlimited hours of entertainment and can provide the answer to pretty much any question ever asked. But it can be a bloated and sometimes lonely place.

We want to remove the fat and isolation whilst placing information directly in your hands.

Our Vision

We want to make digital media fun. Entertaining. Informative. Social. A place where new discoveries are made every day. We don't mean discovering a whole new world or even dinosaurs (although that would be cool too). But new music. New friends and contacts based on common interests.

Why Solid Robot?

In the days of ancient Egypt, Cleopatra was the last active pharaoh of Egypt. Before her death she came across this bright blue metal object. Actually that is not totally true. Whilst she was the last pharoah, she did not come across a bright blue metal object. Well at least we're not aware that she did. That bit was a lie. We got this name from a company name generator that mashed together random words. We liked it and Solid Robot was born. You see, we always believe in the truth.


Established - 2014

We might be a new company but we have the advantage of youth. Fresh ideas, a passion and a desire to shake the internet to it's core.
